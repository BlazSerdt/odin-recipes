Hamburger

Description:

This is a recipe for a fantastic hamburger!

Ingredients:

  • Ground beef
  • Buns
  • Tomatoes
  • Onions
  • Lettuce

Steps:

  1. Cut the buns in half, cut the onions, tomatoes and lettuce.
  2. Shape the beef into hamburger patties and grill them.
  3. Put it all together.
